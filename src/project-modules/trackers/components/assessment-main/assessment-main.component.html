Account Owner Role

There can only be one (1) Account Owner per account. Account Owners have the sole ability to access, manage, and change all account settings. They can access and export account-wide analytics, manage user roles and permissions, curate the account video library, enable additional features in the account, author and manage custom frameworks/rubrics, manage plans and billing, and update colors and logos. Account Owners also have the ability to cancel the account.
